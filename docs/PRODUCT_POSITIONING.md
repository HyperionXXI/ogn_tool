# Product Positioning

## What existing OGN tools do well

- Network-wide coverage views
- Tracking views for aircraft positions
- Range views at a global or regional scale

## What ogn_tool is trying to solve

ogn_tool focuses on local RF diagnostics of a single ground station:

- sector-level analysis
- station quality metrics
- shadow zones
- robust range estimation
- local station comparisons

## Why local logs matter

A local SQLite database enables:

- reproducible analysis
- independence from third‑party services
- comparisons over time
- diagnostics tied to the real installation

## Product positioning

Local RF analysis toolkit for OGN / FLARM / FANET ground stations.

## Intended users

- personal OGN station operators
- paragliding / gliding clubs
- RF hobbyists experimenting with GXAirCom / T-Beam / OGN receivers
- people optimizing antenna placement and site coverage
